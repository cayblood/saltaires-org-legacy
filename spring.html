2015 Spring Show with The Buzz!

The Saltaires on Broadway!


The Saltaires, an internationally top-20-ranked barbershop chorus, is producing its annual a cappella gala event on Saturday, May 16, 2015, at Skyline High School in Salt Lake City, UT.  Headlining the show is The BUZZ, the 2005 Sweet Adelines International quartet champion.


Show times are 2:00 PM and 7:30 PM, with an afterglow (an informal smaller show with chapter and guest quartets and light refreshments) following the evening show. Tickets cost $10 - $20.  Student (K - 12) tickets are only $5 and can be purchased by entering the discount code "student". (Student-rate tickets are only available in the $10 general admission sections. Non-students are on your honor!)


The afterglow costs an additional $5 at the door.


Evening show Golden Circle tickets, which cost $30 and include the front and center seating sections and entrance to the afterglow, can be purchased through chorus members or by contacting tickets@saltaires.org.



Tickets: $10 - 30 http://saltaires2015.brownpapertickets.com/


The Buzz! is the 2005 Sweet Adeline's International Quartet Champions. This dynamic foursome came together in January 2003 and became “the BUZZ” and have been creating just that ever since.

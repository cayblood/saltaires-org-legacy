You can sing too!


Rediscover yourself through harmony

Are you a past or current member of a community chorus or a church ensemble and want to continue having fun honing your singing skills?


Did you ever play in a band or study music in high school or college and now you have no real musical outlet in your life and looking for a musical fix?


Or are you simply looking for a way to have a blast singing with friends and expanding your musical horizons? Now you can! We are looking for good singers with a high degree of musical skill who can support our efforts in providing an emotionally satisfying and entertaining experience for our audiences.


Lend Us Your Voice  

We need men who enjoy A cappella singing (that means no instrumental accompaniment) and would like to lend their voices to strengthen our sound. Our mission is to perfect the performance of barbershop style singing and to be recognized as one of the top barbershop chorus’ in the world.

We welcome guests to our weekly rehearsals. Come join us and audition for the Chorus! Our rehearsals are Wednesday nights at 7:30 p.m. Contact us by email us or call ahead to ensure rehearsal has not been moved to another time, day or location.

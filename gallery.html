Awards, Appreciation, And  Attending A Show

The Saltaires Chorus has received an Outstanding Achievement Award by PROBE. Each year PROBE announces this special recognition for outstanding accomplishments that promoted barbershop harmony in a big way.  These awards are given to districts, chapters, quartets, or individuals based on creativity, impact, exposure and results.


PROBE offered the following description of the award: "The Saltaires Show Chorus appeared on national television, performing at the Provo Freedom Festival in Provo, Utah, during the summer for 14,000 people and a world-wide television broadcast."

Saltaires Receive Coveted PROBE Outstanding Achievement Award

On behalf of Temple Square Performances, I extend our warmest thanks to the Saltaires Show Chorus for the wonderful concert presented in the Assembly Hall.


What can we say that we haven’t said in the past? The Saltaires always put on a great performance and we were so fortunate to feature your group during our summer season on Temple Square. Temple Square Performances Thanks The Saltaires Chorus

If you are in the mood for a purely American musical art form, Barbershop harmony is a style of music created with nothing but human voices coming together to create rich and satisfying vocal textures. The beautifully synchronized sounds are pleasing to the ears and invigorating to the soul. Come enjoy a Saltaires show. Our performances feature a fun filled evening with a wide variety of musical styles including Barbershop, Vocal Jazz, Blues, and Gospel. Often combining with special guests, we sing songs like you've never heard them before. Don't miss an entertaining atmosphere with the Saltaires Chorus. We will fill a music hall with some the world's most harmonic melodies.

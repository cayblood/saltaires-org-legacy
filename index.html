Saltaires - Enriching Lives Through Singing

Our chorus and quartets embrace and perform music predominantly in the barbershop style, and in a broad range of other a cappella styles. Through educational and coaching opportunities available to our chorus, we continually strive to improve our public and contest performances.

Our rehearsals are musically satisfying and fun. We are a diverse group of men, brought together by the enjoyment of harmonizing in the barbershop style. Our chapter performs a cappella music in the barbershop art style as the root of our existence. We strive to enrich and energize members through fellowship, fun, and the joy of singing. With our performances and association with other local music groups, the Saltaires are important contributors to the musical life of the Wasatch Front community.

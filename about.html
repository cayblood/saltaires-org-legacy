Who are the Saltaires?

Taking its name from the historic Utah entertainment site, the Saltaires Show Chorus was formed in 1989 and is located near the beautiful Wasatch Mountain range in Salt Lake City. The Saltaires are one of the best competing barbershop choruses in the world, winning the Rocky Mountain District competition multiple times! The Saltaires have been ranked in the top ten choruses internationally five times in a row and in the top fifteen choruses eight out of the last ten years. The Saltaires have been named by official declaration "The Cultural Ambassadors of the State of Utah" by the Governor of the Great State of Utah!

District & International

The Show Chorus is comprised of about thirty men, singing four-part barbershop harmony music and performing regularly throughout Utah and the Rocky Mountain region. Each year, the Saltaires Chorus competes regionally and internationally in barbershop chorus contests.

The Chorus is a member of the Barbershop Harmony Society (BHS), a non-profit organization formed to preserve the uniquely American musical art form of barbershop quartet singing.

The Saltaires are a professional quality singing organization comprised of volunteer performers, generally gathering together to rehearse just once per week. We encourage all men with a desire and ability to sing to join the Show Chorus. We have had members who "commute" to practice and performances from as far away as Burley, ID and Evanston, WY

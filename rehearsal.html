Saltaires Membership


The Saltaires are always interested in bringing new members into our ranks and we heartily encourage you to visit our rehearsals to learn what our chorus is all about and experience the magic of blending your vocal skills with talented and dedicated singers!


To become a member, you will need to attend and participate in three rehearsals, after which you must pass a voice placement audition, be approved by the Saltaires, and become of member of our parent organization, the Barbershop Harmony Society (www.barbershop.org).
